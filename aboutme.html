Hi there!

My name is Phu and I am really excited to welcome you to my portfolio webpage!

I moved to Northern Territory, Australia from Ho Chi Minh city, Vietnam in November 2020 to pursue my studying at Charles Darwin University (CDU). I was one of 63 international students on board the first charter flight coordinated by CDU. Coming from China, Hong Kong, Japan, Vietnam and Indonesia, we were the first group of international students who have been allowed to enter Australia since the COVID-19 pandemic prompted the nation to shut the borders in Mar 2020. The information about this charter flight can be found in this post. Spending two weeks at the Howard Springs Quarantine Facility, Northern Territory was an amazing experience for me and it was far from boring and isolating thanks to CDU and all the staffs there.

I finished my master degree in Dec 2022 and would like to land a professional job in Australia. Data analytics is my passion and I'm also keen on other jobs requiring analytics skills.

This webpage is a great chance for me to connect people, and share my small projects mostly done during my master course at Charles Darwin University, and some in my spare time, together with my blogs. If you have any comment, I would love to hear from you so please get in touch. Thank you!
