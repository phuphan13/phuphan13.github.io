The 2024 IMO results and analysis

The International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO) is the world most pretigious math competition for high school students, attracting contestants from over 100 countries around the world. The first IMO was held in 1959 in Romania with 7 countries and the latest 2024 IMO is hosted by United Kingdom with 108 countries from 5 continents.

Since 2014, China has continually asserted the dominance securing the positions in top 3, and from 2020 to 2023 China team has maintained its top position but this year USA has taken back the first place. China, USA and South Korea have been remarkably consistent in the top 3 slots.

Kyrgyzstan +49 to 29, New Zealand +33 to 31.



Among the performance of top teams, there are some impressive jumps and significant slips in the standings reflecting the intense competion and rapidly changing in economic landscape.



Each IMO contestant will solve 6 problems (or questions) worth 7 marks each, giving a maximum possible score of 42 marks.
