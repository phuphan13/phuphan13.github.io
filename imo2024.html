The 2024 IMO results and analysis

The International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO) is the world most pretigious math competition for high school students, attracting contestants from over 100 countries around the world. The first IMO in Romania in 1959 with 7 countries and the latest 2024 IMO is hosted by United Kingdom with 108 countries from 5 continents.

Since 2014, China has continually asserted the dominance securing its positions in top 3. Between 2020 to 2024, China, USA and South Korea teams have been remarkably consistent in their performance, China has maintain top position in four consecutive years before USA has taken back the first place this year.



Within the top ten, there are some impressive growth, Poland jumped from the 32nd in 2023 to the 9th in 2024, Romania from 27th to 5th in 2022. Meanwhile, some countries were not able to maintain or improve their standings. Notably, Vietnam experienced a significant decline, plummeting from 7th in 2023 to 33rd in 2024.
